Distracker

Distracted by social media? Let us help you track your social media usage.

About

Meet Doodle!

Are you always checking social media, sports or other applications on your mobile phone? You are not alone! Doodle is fed up with all of the time he spends on his mobile phone! He is ready to make a change, measure his mobile device usage, and minimize the amount of time he procrastinates! But how will he do that...... With Distracker! Distracker is an application that measures how much time an individual spends on distracting social media applications!

Next Steps

How It Works:

  • Runs securely in the background on your mobile device, measuring and reporting the time an individual spends on social media applications on a daily, weekly and monthly basis

Features:

  • Notifies an individual how much time they have spent on social media applications
  • Logs the individuals daily, weekly, and monthly usage

Next Steps:

  • Expand the distractment tracker to all applications, including: all social media, game, sports, and any other applications
  • Add a blocking application feature which allows the user to block usage on an application for a certain period of time or to block them completely
  • Add notifications to inform users of how much time they are spending on certain applications
  • Set goals in order to reduce the time an individual spends on their mobile device
  • Set alarms when an individual spends more than x amount of hours on a certain application
  • Send weekly emails summarizing the users activities and productivity for that week
  • Expand to businesses and allow them to use our data log of all of the users to identify how much time users spend on each application
