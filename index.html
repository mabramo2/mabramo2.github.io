Distracker

Distracted by social media? Let us help you track your social media usage.

About

Meet Doodle!

Are you constantly finding yourself checking social media, sports or other applications on your cell phone? You are not alone! Doodle is fed up with all of the time he spends on his cell phone! He is ready to make a change and measure how much time he actually spends doing these things, in the hopes of minimizing the time he spends getting distracted! But how will he do that...... With Distracker! Distracker is an application available to measure how much time you spend on distracting social media applications, sports applications, etc.

Next Steps

How It Works:

  • Runs securely in the background on your mobile device, measuring and reporting the time you spent on applications on a daily, weekly and monthly basis

Features:

  • Notifies you how much time you have spent on email, messaging, social media, sports, game and all other applications
  • Logs your daily usage

Next Steps:

  • Add a blocking application feature which allows the user to block usage on an application for a certain period of time or to block them completely
  • Add notifications to inform users of how much time they are spending on certain applications
  • Set goals in order to reduce the time spent on your mobile device
  • Set alarms when you spend more than x hours on a certain application
  • Send weekly emails summarizing the users activities and productivity for that week
  • Expand to businesses and allow them to use our data log of all of the users to identify how much time users spend on each application
